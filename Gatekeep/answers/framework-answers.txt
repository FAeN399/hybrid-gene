MONSTER BATTLER - FRAMEWORK ANSWERS
====================================

## Core Fantasy

**What's the player fantasy?**
Monster breeding and training and battling.

**What makes this different from Pokemon/Digimon/Monster Rancher?**
Fast paced action packed battles.

**What's the "hell yeah" moment you want players to experience?**
Finding a cool combination of breeding, using awesome abilities, and using fun tactics and timing with your monsters.

## Combat Feel

**What existing game's combat feels closest to what you want?**
Digimon World 1, but faster paced.

**During a fight, is the player a coach, a commander, or a participant?**
Commander and coach.

## Monster Relationship

**Are monsters partners or tools?**
Partners, temperament will be altered by relationship.

**How often should players retire/replace monsters?**
There will be quite a few monsters the player will want, perhaps starting with a fence that can hold 6 and then you can upgrade your base to hold more, maybe 20 max.

## Progression

**Where does power come from?**
Battling increases power, as well as parents passing stronger stats and abilities to offspring.

**Should progression feel like a grind, a puzzle, or a journey?**
The progression is a mix of grind and journey.

## Priorities

**If you could only ship 3 features, what are they?**
Breeding, Combat, Arena.

**What gets cut first if scope becomes a problem?**
Base building and item crafting.

**Anything else on your mind about the framework?**
Nope.
